Focus Flower

Growing better beliefs

Instructions

Begin by choosing a thought or feeling that you’d like to improve. This will be the starting point of your focus wheel. Type your starting thought in the first entry field to the right and press Enter. For the next entry, look for a thought that feels just a bit better than the previous one. Small shifts are key here—find something that creates a bridge to a slightly more positive perspective. Keep adding small, better-feeling thoughts in each entry space. Each thought should feel like a natural step forward from the last. As you complete each entry, it will be recorded in the focus flower to the left, helping you see your progression toward a more uplifting mindset. By the end, you’ll have created a series of thoughts that lead to a more empowered and positive perspective on your original thought.
